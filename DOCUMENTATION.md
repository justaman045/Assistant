## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Go to the homepage and click **Sign in with Google**.
2. You will be taken through a short onboarding flow — enter your preferred name and role (e.g. "Content Creator", "Founder").
3. Once onboarding is complete you land on the main Dashboard.

New accounts receive **50,000 free tokens** — enough to try every feature.

### Navigation

The left sidebar is your main navigation. On mobile, tap the hamburger menu at the top to open the drawer.

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your activity hub. It shows:

- **Token balance** — how many tokens you have remaining and have used.
- **Recent content** — your last few generated pieces with quick links to open or edit them.
- **Usage stats** — a chart of your generation activity over time.
- **Model breakdown** — which AI models you've used and how often.

## 3. AI Apps

**Path:** Sidebar → Apps

Apps are guided, form-based templates that produce a specific output type. Fill in the structured fields and click Generate — the AI handles the rest.

### How Apps Work

1. Select an app from the gallery.
2. Fill in the form fields (required ones are marked).
3. Choose your AI model from the model picker (optional — defaults to Gemini 2.0 Flash).
4. Click **Generate**. Output streams in real time.
5. When done, click **Save** to store it in your Content History, or copy it directly.

## 4. Content Creation (Free-form)

**Path:** Sidebar → Create

For when the structured Apps don't fit your needs. Type or paste any prompt and generate content without constraints.

- Choose your content type from a dropdown (Blog Post, Tweet, Email, etc.) or type a custom one.
- Set a target length (words or characters).
- Pick your AI model.
- Click **Generate** — output streams live.

Generated content can be saved to History with a title you choose.

## 5. Content History & Versions

**Path:** Sidebar → History

Every piece of saved content lives here.

### Managing Items

- **Search** — filter by title or content.
- **Open** — view or edit any saved item.
- **Delete** — permanently remove an item.

### Version History

Every time you regenerate or manually edit a saved item, a new version is created automatically.

- Click **Versions** on any item to see the full history.
- Each version is numbered and labelled with its source (initial, edit, regenerated, restored).
- Click **Restore** on any past version to roll back to it.

## 6. AI Assistants

**Path:** Sidebar → Assistants

Create named AI assistants with their own personality, system prompt, and preferred model. Each assistant maintains separate chat histories.

### Creating an Assistant

1. Click **New Assistant**.
2. Give it a name, choose an emoji avatar, and write a personality description.
3. Optionally write a custom system prompt to give it specific instructions.
4. Choose a default AI model.
5. Click **Save**.

### Chatting

- Select an assistant to open its chat view.
- Start a new conversation or pick up an existing one from the sidebar.
- Messages stream in real time.
- Each conversation is saved automatically.

Assistants can use tools — when the assistant performs an action (like searching or summarising), you'll see a tool activity log beneath its response.

## 7. Roleplay Partners

**Path:** Sidebar → Roleplay

Create AI conversation partners with distinct personas — useful for practice, creative writing, marketing role-play, or entertainment.

### Creating a Partner

1. Click **New Partner**.
2. Set the name, persona description, and personality traits.
3. Choose a category: General, Marketing, Therapy, NSFW, or Custom.
4. Toggle **Memory** on to let the partner remember things across conversations.
5. Choose a model and click **Save**.

### Memory Toggle

When memory is enabled, the partner extracts and stores facts from your conversations and injects them into future sessions — creating a sense of continuity.

## 9. Finance Tracker

**Path:** Sidebar → Finance

Log income and expenses and ask the AI questions about your spending patterns.

### Adding Transactions

- Click **Add Transaction**.
- Enter amount, type (Income / Expense), category, description, and date.

The AI has access to your full transaction history for accurate answers.

## 13. Memory System

**Path:** Sidebar → Memory

The Memory system lets the AI learn your preferences, writing style, and context over time — automatically.

### How It Works

After each AI-powered generation, a background process analyses the content and extracts facts worth remembering (your tone preferences, recurring topics, things you've mentioned about yourself). These are stored as memory entries and injected into future AI prompts across every feature.

### Viewing & Managing Memories

- **Memory page** lists all stored entries with their source and category.
- You can delete any memory you don't want the AI to carry forward.
- Memories are private — they live in your account only.

### Categories

Memories are tagged by type: Writing Style, Personal Context, Topic Preferences, etc.

## 14. Brand Voice

**Path:** Sidebar → Brand Voice (or Settings → Brand Voice)

Define how you want to sound — and every piece of AI-generated content will reflect it.

### Configuration

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Tone** | e.g. Professional, Casual, Witty, Authoritative |
| **Style** | e.g. Concise, Story-driven, Data-heavy |
| **Target Audience** | Who you're writing for |
| **Words to Avoid** | Phrases or words you never use |
| **Sample Phrase** | A sentence that sounds like you |

Once saved, Brand Voice is automatically injected into the system prompt for all content generation — you don't need to set it per request.

## 15. Billing & Tokens

**Path:** Sidebar → Billing

### How Tokens Work

All AI features consume tokens — the currency of Large Language Models. You are charged only for what you actually use (input + output tokens combined), not a flat rate per generation.

- Your balance is shown in the top bar.
- A warning appears when you're running low (below 10,000 tokens).
- Generations are blocked if your balance falls below 100 tokens.

### Plans

| Plan | Price | Monthly Tokens | Key Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | ₹0 | 50,000 | All models, content history, memory |
| **Starter** | ₹299/month | 500,000 | Brand Voice, export (MD, TXT) |
| **Pro** | ₹699/month | 1,500,000 | Brand Voice, referral bonuses, calendar |
| **Business** | ₹1,499/month | 5,000,000 | Everything + dedicated support |

Annual billing saves up to 30% compared to monthly.

### Token Packs (Pay-as-you-go)

If you run out mid-month, buy a top-up — no plan change needed.

| Pack | Tokens | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Starter Pack | 100,000 | ₹99 |
| Pro Pack | 500,000 | ₹399 |
| Power Pack | 2,000,000 | ₹1,299 |

### Upgrading or Cancelling

Manage your plan from the Billing page. Subscriptions are processed via Razorpay — you can cancel at any time and retain access until the end of your billing period.

## 18. Admin Panel

**Path:** `/admin` (requires `isAdmin: true` on your user account)

The Admin Panel is for platform operators only — not end users.

### Stats

Overview of total users, active users, token consumption, and revenue.

### Users

- List all registered users with their plan, token balance, and join date.
- Click any user to see their profile, adjust their token balance, or change their plan.

### API Keys

Manage OpenRouter API keys used for all AI requests:

- Add multiple keys with labels.
- Set the active key — all AI requests route through it.
- Switch keys instantly without redeploying (updates propagate within 1 minute via server-side cache).
- "Use env var" option falls back to the `OPENROUTER_API_KEY` environment variable.

### Model Usage

Detailed log of which AI models were used, by whom, and how many tokens each request consumed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which AI models are available?**
Any model available on OpenRouter — including GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, Gemini 2.0 Flash, Llama 3, Mistral, and hundreds more. The model picker on each page shows the full list with pricing and context window size.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. All content, memories, tasks, and transactions are stored in your private Firestore account. Firestore security rules enforce strict owner-only access — no other user can read your data.

**What happens if I run out of tokens?**
Generation is paused until you top up. You can buy a token pack from the Billing page at any time — they're instant and don't affect your subscription.

**Can I export my data?**
Yes. Go to Settings → Account Export to download all your content and data as a JSON file.

**How does Brand Voice work with AI?**
When you save your Brand Voice settings, they're appended to the system prompt for every AI generation — instructing the model to match your tone, style, and audience. You don't need to paste it manually into each request.

**Is there a mobile app?**
The web app is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. There is no native app at this time.
